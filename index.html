WELCOME

Subaru TEAM,

I am excited, and grateful, to be assuming my new role at Subaru of America Inc, (SOA). I’m appreciative for Tom Doll’s guidance and mentorship, as well as his leadership to position Subaru for continued success. I also am thankful to our Subaru Retailers for your help and support. I appreciate all of you very much!

As we all know, the last three years have been challenging, but if recent production forecasts are any indication, we are hopeful we may be emerging from our most challenging days and moving toward a significant sales recovery in 2023.

Read More

All of us at Subaru Corporation (SBR) and Subaru of America Inc, are working hard to overcome the production and supply chain challenges that still exist and, like you, are looking forward to getting our great brand back on offense. In fact, we are forecasting retail sales of 600,000 vehicles in 2023, which will represent an increase of 8% over 2022 sales of 556,581 vehicles.

Our marketing theme for this year is “Subaru… Navigating the Stars!” This theme is appropriate because we are not only going to be navigating our recovery, but we will also begin to show you how we will be navigating our transition to hybrids and EV models that are going to be introduced in greater volumes as we move into the CY25/26 periods. The Solterra is just the start of a new product strategy with our EV models. We’re also making plans on how to handle the transition to an increased level of autonomous driving technology, connected vehicles, and how our future owners want to shop, purchase, and use their Subaru.

That’s why we’d like to invite you to our 2023 National Business Conference (NBC), in Nashville, Tennessee. Our NBC will be held from Monday, July 10 through Wednesday, July 12. While we’re there, in addition to providing a future business update, we will be sure to experience all the fun that Music City has to offer. You can enroll on this website now!

In addition to recognizing you, our Subaru Retailers, for the outstanding work you have done to live up to our Subaru Love Promise with your customers and in your communities, we will also provide you with detailed updates that are vital to your continued success:

  • We will showcase the new products that are headed your way, including an update on our EV strategy.
  • We will provide updated information on sales and production.
  • We will introduce Jeff Williams, from Williams Subaru in Lansing, Michigan, the new chair of our National Retail Advisory Board.
  • You will learn about how we are evolving our marketing and advertising strategies for the coming year.
  • You will hear from Tady Yoshida, SOA’s CEO, and his vision regarding Subaru’s future.
  • And, of course, we’ll have the opportunity to thank Tom Doll for his selfless dedication to our brand, his leadership on how we do business and partner with our Retailers, as well as have him deliver his final keynote address.

In addition to learning, networking, and socializing, we also live up to our Subaru Love Promise by giving back to the communities that host our conferences. If you haven’t seen it, be sure to take a look at our 2022 NBC “Austin Donations Video” to see all the good we’ve done at our NBC meetings over the years, and especially last year in Austin, Texas.

The last three years have taught us that together, we have built one of the strongest brands in the auto industry or any industry. Let’s keep working together to build our brand and your franchise.

The continued success and strength of our company is a credit to you – our Retailers – for embracing the Subaru Love Promise, demonstrating that you are more than a typical car dealer and showing that Subaru is More Than a Car Company®.

We are Subaru… and together, we are… Navigating the Stars!!

We look forward to seeing you in Nashville!

It’s great to be a part of a winning team!

Jeffrey A. Walters, President and COO
